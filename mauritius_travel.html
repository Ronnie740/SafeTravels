Labourdonnais Waterfront Hotel

Labourdonnais Waterfront Hotel provides comfortable 5-star accommodation in Port Louis. Guests also have exclusive access to the ISpa Fitness & Wellness Club and ISpa Fitness & Wellness Club. Labourdonnais Waterfront Hotel Port Louis has been recently refurbished and offers an outdoor pool, a sauna and free Wi-Fi. There is also free private parking on site for those travelling with a car.

Four Seasons Mauritius

With a stay at Four Seasons Mauritius at Anahita in Grand River South East, you'll be within a 10-minute walk of Ile aux Cerfs Golf Club and Ile aux Cerfs Beach. Featured amenities include a 24-hour business center, express check-in, and express check-out. Planning an event in Grand River South East? This resort features 1550 square feet (140 square meters) of event facilities.

The Residence Mauritius

When you stay at The Residence Mauritius in Belle Mare, you'll be on the beach, within a 5-minute drive of Belle Mare Beach and Palmar Beach. Featured amenities include complimentary wired Internet access, a business center, and express check-out. A roundtrip airport shuttle is provided for a surcharge (available 24 hours), and free valet parking is available onsite.
