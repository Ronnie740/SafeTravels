Entumoto Safari Camp

Entumoto is an elegant, authentic, stylish camp with a history going back to the 1920's. This family owned safari camp is run by Swedish and Maasai families. It is situated in a beautifully secluded valley overlooking the Masai Mara. It has a unique position within its own wildlife conservancy with direct access to the park.

Mara Intrepids Tented Camp

Overlooking the Talek River, at the very confluence of Mara’s four game-viewing areas, Mara Intrepids Camp enjoys one of the most spectacular locations in the world’s best-known wilderness.

Learn More

Elewana Sand River Masai Mara

Located on the banks of a river from which it has taken its name, Sand River Masai Mara is located within the Masai Mara National Reserve, close to the Tanzanian border. Situated on a secluded and picturesque site, the camp replicates the heyday of exclusive permanent tented camps of the late 1920’s. The interior fixtures and furnishings mirror this period perfectly, reminding all who visit of the countless Hollywood movies that have paid homage to a classic era of African adventure.
